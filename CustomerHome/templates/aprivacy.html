Privacy Policy

At Thunder Car & Bike Rental, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our services. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information.

Information We Collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, contact information (email address, phone number, address), and identification documents (driver’s license, passport) required for rental purposes.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details or other payment information for processing rental transactions.
  • Device and Usage Information: Technical information such as IP address, device type, browser type, and operating system when you access our website or use our services.
  • Location Information: If you provide us with location data or allow access to your device’s location settings, we may collect this information for specific rental services.
  • Communications: We may record and store any communications you have with our customer support or through our website.

How We Use Your Information:

  • Providing Rental Services: To process your reservations, communicate with you, and manage the rental transaction.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, provide assistance, and address any issues you may encounter.
  • Improving Services: To enhance our website, services, and offerings based on user preferences and feedback.
  • Marketing: With your consent, we may send promotional offers, updates, and newsletters related to our services.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.
